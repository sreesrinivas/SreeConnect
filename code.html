Code of Conduct

Effective Date: May 10, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to SreeConnect! Our mission is to provide high-quality educational resources for BTech 1st Year students. We are committed to maintaining a positive, inclusive, and respectful environment for all users. This Code of Conduct outlines the standards and expectations for behavior on our platform.

Respect and Inclusivity

We believe every user deserves to be treated with dignity and respect. SreeConnect is an inclusive community where all individuals—regardless of their background, gender, ethnicity, nationality, or educational level—are welcomed and supported.

  • Be kind and courteous in all interactions.
  • Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
  • Avoid discriminatory language, hate speech, or personal attacks.

Academic Integrity

We encourage students to use SreeConnect resources to learn and understand their academic material better. However, users must maintain academic honesty and integrity:

  • Do not plagiarize materials or present others' work as your own.
  • Use resources responsibly to aid learning, not for cheating or unethical practices.
  • If sharing our resources with others, always give appropriate credit.

Content Usage

All notes, textbooks, model papers, assignments, and lab materials provided on SreeConnect are intended solely for educational purposes. Unauthorized reproduction, resale, or distribution of content without permission is strictly prohibited.

User Behavior

When interacting with the SreeConnect community, either through comments, feedback forms, or social media, users must:

  • Communicate politely and constructively.
  • Refrain from spamming, advertising, or promoting unrelated services.
  • Report any inappropriate behavior or content to the administrators.

Reporting Violations

If you encounter any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact us at support@sreeconnect.com. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

Consequences of Misconduct

Failure to adhere to this Code of Conduct may result in actions including, but not limited to:

  • Removal of access to certain parts of the website.
  • Permanent banning from SreeConnect.
  • Notification to educational institutions, if necessary, in cases of academic dishonesty.

Amendments

SreeConnect reserves the right to update or modify this Code of Conduct at any time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. We encourage users to review this page periodically.

Thank You

Thank you for being a part of SreeConnect. Together, we can build a vibrant, respectful, and academically honest community that helps everyone achieve success!
